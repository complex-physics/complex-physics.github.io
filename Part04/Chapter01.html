1. Basic idea of HHL

1.1. Question

The goal of HHL is solve the linear equation Ax⃗=b⃗ A \vec{x}=\vec{b} Ax=b where

  • matrix AAA and a vector b⃗\vec{b}b are given
  • find x⃗\vec{x} x satisfying this linear equation

we can convert the equation into x⃗=A−1b⃗ \vec{x}=A^{-1} \vec{b} x=A−1b

1.3.2. Quantum Phase Estimation

Quantum Phase Estimation (QPE) has three components

  1. the superposition of the clock qubits through Hadamard gates,
  2. controlled rotation
  3. Inverse Quantum Fourier Transform (IQFT). The goal of QPE is to estimate the phase of the eigenvalues of the unitary rotation matrix, U=eiAtU=e^{i A t}U=eiAt, in the controlled gate, C−UC-UC−U, (Fig. 1) used in the QPE. Again, this gate encodes the matrix AAA
